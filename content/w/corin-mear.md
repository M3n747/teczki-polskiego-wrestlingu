## Professional wrestling career

### Dream Factory Wrestling (2015-2017)

Corin Mear was one of the founding members of [Dream Factory Wrestling](@/o/dfw.md), a backyard federation based in the Toru≈Ñ area. He made his debut at DFW's [first ever show](@/e/dfw/2015-06-20-dfw-showcase.md) in June 2015. He teamed up with [Malmo Buruto](@/w/malmo-buruto.md) to defeat [Pain Killer](@/w/pain-killer.md) and [Charlie Greyson](@/w/madman-charlie.md) in a Tag Team Match. He stood out from the rest of the roster by wearing facepaint. He worked mostly in midcard, facing wrestlers such as [PJ Blake](@/w/pj-blake.md) and [Chris Hunter](@/w/chris-hunter.md). During [Tournament of Dreams](@/e/dfw/2016-06-11-dfw-tournament-of-dreams-1.md) he advanced to Semi Finals in a tournament to crown the inaugrual [DFW Champion](@/c/dfw-championship.md), but he was defeated by Chris Hunter.

In total, Corin made 16 appearances for DFW, with his last one taking place at [Dream More and Never Stop](@/e/dfw/2017-12-09-dfw-dream-more-and-never-stop.md) in December 2017, where he defeated [Cade Bruce](@/w/mister-z.md) in a No DQ Match. After that he seemingly retired from wrestling.
